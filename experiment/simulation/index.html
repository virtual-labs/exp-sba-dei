🚀 Quick Start Guide

  1. Create Services: Click on "Service" in the left panel to add new services with custom names, IPs, and ports
  2. Auto-Connection: New services automatically connect to the main bus line and existing services
  3. Move Bus Line: Drag the bus line to reposition it on the canvas
  4. Configure Bus: Click on the bus line to rename it, change its color, or adjust its length
  5. Bus Communication: Services communicate through the bus line for efficient routing
  6. Manual Connections: Use "Select Source" → "Select Destination" for direct connections between services and buses
  7. Monitor Logs: View service initialization, bus connections, and request/response logs

🔗 Connection Types

  • Service → Bus: Automatic connection to main bus line
  • Service → Service: Direct service-to-service connections
  • Bus → Bus: Connect multiple bus lines together
  • Bus-Routed: Communication through bus lines
